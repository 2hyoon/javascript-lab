Accordion

An accordion is a user interface element that allows users to show and hide sections of related content. It's a vertically stacked list of items, where each item can be "expanded" or "collapsed" to reveal the content associated with it.

Accordions are useful for progressively disclosing information to users, reducing clutter on the page. They are particularly effective for FAQs, product features, or any content that can be broken down into distinct sections, helping users focus on the information most relevant to them.

Yes, when implemented correctly with ARIA attributes. Key attributes include aria-expanded on the button to indicate state, aria-controls to link the button to its content panel, and appropriate roles like region and aria-labelledby on the content panel for screen reader users.
